Curried Egg Sandwich


Photo from allrecipes.com

Have a delicious egg sandwich to start your day

It is easy and quick to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 4 hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped
  • Half a cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 slices bread

Prepare the dish

  1. Gather your Ingredients
  2. Mix and stir eggs and mayonnaise in a bowl
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Evenly divide between 4 slices of bread, top with remaining 4 slices
